Law Title 1: The draft law for the amendment of the Law on hunting and the protection of the hunting fund no. 55/2024 (art. 2, 3`1)

This law proposes to amend the Law "Hunting and the protection of the hunting stock" no. 55/2024, introducing the notion of "regional hunting fund of UTA Gagauzia" and regulating the particularities of its management. The aim is to align regional and national competences, increasing the efficiency of the administration of hunting funds and the protection of fauna in Gagauzia, in accordance with local and national legislation.

Law Title 2: The draft law for the accession of the Republic of Moldova to the fourth additional Protocol to the General Regulation of the Universal Postal Union, drawn up in Riyadh on October 5, 2023

Decision no. 824 of December 11 2024, of the Government of the Republic of Moldova approves the draft law for accession to the fourth additional Protocol to the Regulation of the Universal Postal Union, adopted in Riyadh on October 5, 2023. The accession will allow the Republic of Moldova to comply with international regulations in the postal field . The Government will take the necessary measures for its implementation, and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs will submit the accession instrument. The law will enter into force after publication in the Official Gazette.

Law Title 3: The draft law for the accession of the Republic of Moldova to the first Additional Protocol to the Universal Postal Convention, drawn up in Riyadh on October 5, 2023

Decision No. 826 of December 11 2024, of the Government of the Republic of Moldova approves the draft law for the accession to the first Additional Protocol to the Universal Postal Convention, drawn up in Riyadh on October 5, 2023. The accession will allow the Republic of Moldova to comply with the new international regulations in the field of postal services . The Government will take the necessary measures to implement the Protocol, and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs will submit the accession instrument. The law will enter into force after publication in the Official Gazette.

Law Title 4: The draft law for the amendment of annex no. 10 to Law no. 270/2018 regarding the unitary payroll system in the budget sector

The draft law for the amendment of annex no. 10 to Law no. 270/2018 regarding the unitary salary system in the budget sector proposes the updating of the salary class for the position of meteorologist and the introduction of new functions specific to the hydro-meteorological field. These changes are aimed at improving the remuneration of employees of the State Hydro-meteorological Service, ensuring a fair and transparent system of staff remuneration. The changes also aim to attract and retain specialists in this essential field for weather risk forecasting and management.

Law Title 5: The draft law for the acceptance of the Protocol amending the Marrakesh Agreement on the establishment of the World Trade Organization, drawn up in Geneva on 17 June 2022

The Law Approving the Protocol Amending the Marrakesh Agreement Establishing the World Trade Organization, adopted in Geneva on June 17, 2022, aims to update and improve the organization's structure to respond more effectively to today's trade challenges. The Protocol aims to modernize dispute settlement mechanisms and strengthen the WTO's capacity to support international trade in a fairer and more sustainable way. Thus, this change will strengthen member states' commitments to WTO rules and contribute to more efficient global trade.

Law Title 7: The draft law on the exemption from customs duty, the duty for carrying out customs procedures and the value added tax without the right to deduct goods and services imported and/or delivered for the agricultural sector of UTA Gagauzia

The proposed law on the exemption from the payment of customs duties, the tax for carrying out customs procedures and the value added tax, without the right of deduction, for the goods and services imported and/or delivered in the agricultural sector of UTA Gagauzia aims to support the development of this sector by reducing fiscal costs. The measures will help stimulate local agricultural production and support farmers in the region, facilitating their access to necessary resources. The law also aims to improve the competitiveness of agriculture in Gagauzia on the domestic and international market.

Law Title 8: The draft law to amend the Law on Vine and Wine no. 57/2006 (art. 1, 2, 3, etc.)

Law amending the Law on Vine and Wine no. 57/2006 aims to align the legislation of the Republic of Moldova with the regulations of the European Union in the wine sector. Among the important changes are the definition and regulation of wine establishments, the adaptation of measures on the internal market and the promotion of wine products, as well as the adjustment of requirements for small wine producers. Also, the law proposes new definitions for various categories of producers and wine-growing activities, as well as the harmonization of some administrative procedures.

Law Title 9: The draft law for the amendment of some normative acts (the effective fight against the phenomenon of electoral corruption and its related aspects)

The law on the amendment of some normative acts (combating electoral corruption and related aspects) refers to the introduction of stricter measures in the legislation of the Republic of Moldova to combat the phenomenon of electoral corruption. Among the important changes is the updating of the Criminal Code and the Law on special investigative activity, to strengthen the sanctions applicable to organized criminal groups and legal entities involved in such activities. Extremist activities are also regulated more severely, the prohibition of extremist organizations and the dissemination of materials of an extremist nature.

Law Title 10: The draft law to amend Law no. 50/2008 on the protection of inventions (consolidation of the normative framework applicable to the protection of inventions)

The draft law to amend Law no. 50/2008 on the protection of inventions aims to strengthen the normative framework applicable to the protection of inventions in the Republic of Moldova. The law transposes several European regulations, including Directive 2004/48/EC on the respect of intellectual property rights and regulations on supplementary protection certificates for pharmaceutical and phytosanitary products. The changes include clarifications related to the protection of biotechnological inventions, the granting of compulsory licenses and the protection of European patents.

Law Title 11: Horticulture draft law

The Horticulture Law of the Republic of Moldova establishes a detailed legal framework for the regulation of the horticulture sector. It aims to increase the production and productivity of horticultural products, supporting the use of advanced technologies and promoting sustainable development. By harmonizing with European regulations and encouraging investments, the law aims to diversify and adapt products to the requirements of domestic and foreign markets. Implementation is ensured by the Ministry of Agriculture and the Horticultural Office, which coordinates policies, research and funding measures in the sector.

Law Title 12: The draft law on civil status documents

This law regulates in detail the process of registration of civil status documents in the Republic of Moldova. It sets out the competent authorities, such as the Public Services Agency and local public administrations, as well as the procedures for birth, marriage, divorce, death and other events. It also provides for the use of an information system for the management and preservation of documents, providing the legal framework for the validation of documents in relation to other states.
